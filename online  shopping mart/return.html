Cancellation

Cancellations of orders is possible only before the order is invoiced for shipment. Once an order is invoiced and packed, it cannot be cancelled
To cancel an order, please call our Customer Support Center @ 022-41117877 and give your order number requesting for a cancellation. If the order hasn’t been invoiced, our team will cancel the order for you and the refund will be posted back via the same method of payment for all pre-paid orders.
